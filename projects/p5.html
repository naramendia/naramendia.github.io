Marching Squares: Revealing Geography

Using p5.js, I designed a series of short animations for a website landing page for my team at Esri and LinkedIn recruiting posts. Each animation layers maps from different locations around the world, drawing on historic, topographic, and satellite imagery. I implemented the Marching Squares algorithm for controlled randomness to gradually reveal the underlying geographic layers.
