Power of Where Storyboards

During my internship at Esri, I designed and iterated on a series of low-fidelity cartographic storyboards to support the development of the *Power of Where* web experience. The *Power of Where* page demonstrates how GIS can be used to solve real-world challenges through three scenarios: prioritizing infrastructure investments, anticipating market changes to support business expansion, and reducing operational disruptions during critical events. The storyboards served as a communication and alignment tool throughout the design process, helping stakeholders visualize narrative flow and animation sequences before production.
